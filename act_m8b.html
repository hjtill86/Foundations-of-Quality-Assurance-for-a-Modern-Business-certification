Activity 8B — Vantage Healthcare Audit Report

← Back to Course Hub
🎯 Learning Objective: Interpret an internal audit report, write formal nonconformance findings, and develop an audit action plan.

Scenario

You are the lead internal auditor at Vantage Healthcare Supplies. You have just completed a two-day internal audit of the warehouse and production departments. Your notes from the audit include:
  • Training records for 4 of 11 warehouse staff are missing or incomplete
  • The current version of the Goods Inward Inspection SOP (v3.2) is not posted at the inspection station — v2.1 is still displayed
  • The pest control log has not been signed for 6 weeks, though contractor says inspections were done
  • All calibration records for measuring equipment are current and correctly filed
  • One team leader could not name the correct escalation path for a product hold situation

Question 1 — Write Two Formal Findings

Write two formal audit findings from the notes above. Each finding must include: (a) Observation, (b) ISO 9001 clause reference, (c) Severity rating.

Question 2 — Positive Finding

Identify one area of conformance observed during the audit and explain why it represents good practice.

Question 3 — Audit Action Plan

Create an action plan for the two findings you wrote. For each: assign responsibility, define the corrective action, and set a target completion date.

Model Answer

1Finding 1: 4/11 incomplete training records → Clause 7.2 (Competence) → Minor NC. Finding 2: Obsolete SOP at workstation → Clause 7.5.3 → Major NC (obsolete document in active use).
2Positive: Calibration records fully current. Good practice because it demonstrates disciplined equipment management, protects measurement integrity, and signals a mature QMS.
3Action Plan: Training records — HR Manager, 30-day completion. Obsolete SOP — Document Controller, 48-hour removal and site walk for all workstations. Include follow-up verification date for each.
