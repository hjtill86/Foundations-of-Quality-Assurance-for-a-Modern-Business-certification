Activity 3B — Compliance Gap Scenario Analysis

← Back to Course Hub
🎯 Learning Objective: Identify compliance gaps in a real-world scenario and recommend corrective actions aligned with ISO 9001 requirements.

Scenario

GreenPack Manufacturing produces sustainable packaging for the food industry. They received ISO 9001:2015 certification two years ago but have not conducted an internal audit since certification. Their quality policy was approved by the previous CEO and has never been reviewed. Staff turnover has been 30% over the past year, but training records are not consistently maintained. A major customer has requested an on-site supplier audit next month.

Question 1 — Identify the Compliance Gaps

List at least three specific compliance gaps in GreenPack's current situation and identify which ISO 9001:2015 clause each gap violates.

Question 2 — Corrective Actions

For each gap you identified, recommend a specific corrective action GreenPack should take before the customer audit.

Question 3 — Priority Assessment

Which gap poses the greatest risk to GreenPack in the upcoming customer audit and why?

Model Answer

1Gap 1: No internal audit since certification — violates Clause 9.2. Action: Conduct an urgent internal audit of all core processes before the customer visit.
2Gap 2: Quality policy not reviewed since change in leadership — violates Clause 5.2. Action: Schedule a leadership review of the quality policy; update and reissue with current CEO approval.
3Gap 3: Inconsistent training records despite 30% staff turnover — violates Clause 7.2 (Competence). Action: Audit all employee files; complete missing records and conduct catch-up training for any gaps found.
★Greatest Risk: Missing training records — a customer auditor will almost certainly sample training records for new staff. Missing records for 30% of the workforce creates an immediate major nonconformance risk.
