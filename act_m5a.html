🎯 Learning Objective: Build a risk register by scoring likelihood and impact, calculating risk ratings, and assigning appropriate controls.

Scenario

You are the QA Manager at Apex Logistics. Leadership has asked you to complete a risk register for the warehouse order fulfillment process. Five risk scenarios have been identified. Complete each row.

Risk Scoring Guide

Likelihood
1 = Rare   2 = Unlikely   3 = Possible   4 = Likely   5 = Almost Certain
Impact
1 = Negligible   2 = Minor   3 = Moderate   4 = Major   5 = Catastrophic
Risk Rating = Likelihood × Impact  |  1-5 = Low  |  6-12 = Medium  |  13-25 = High
